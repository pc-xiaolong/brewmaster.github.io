Common Questions

Everything you need
to know

Is BrewMaster Academy really free to start? +
Yes — completely free with no credit card required. The free plan includes all 35+ coffee drink recipes, all 13 bean origin profiles, the AI barista chat, component checker, glossary, and study notes. The only thing behind the Pro plan is the structured Barista Academy (15 lessons), progress tracking, and achievement badges.
Do I need any equipment to benefit? +
Not at all. The recipes cover everything from a $15 moka pot to professional espresso machines. Whether you have a French press, pour over dripper, or a full espresso setup — there's deep content for every level. Many people start with basic equipment and upgrade gradually as their skills grow.
What makes the AI barista different from just searching Google? +
The AI barista (powered by Claude) is specifically trained as a coffee expert — a Q-grader, barista, and educator in one. It gives passionate, specific, actionable answers rather than generic results. It understands context (e.g. "why is my espresso sour?") and gives a targeted diagnosis rather than a list of unrelated articles.
How does the Component Checker work? +
Select a coffee drink and describe the ingredients you have or plan to use. The AI validates your ingredients against the correct recipe, scores your preparation, identifies any issues or substitutions needed with a live animated analysis, and suggests specific fixes. It also recommends variations to try once you've nailed the base recipe.
Can I cancel Pro at any time? +
Yes — cancel any time with no questions asked. Your progress and study notes are saved and accessible on the free plan forever, even after cancelling Pro. We offer a 7-day free trial so you can experience the full curriculum before committing.
